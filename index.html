I am an Assistant Professor in the department of Software at Korea Aerospace University. My research interests involve connecting the areas of Machine Learning and High Performance Computing—more specifically, parallelization of Deep Neural Network. I am also interested in applications in natural language processing, bioinformatics, activity recognition, and general AI.

Before joining KAU, I received my Ph.D. from the Ohio State University and was a postdoctoral researcher in the Center for Computing Research at Sandia National Laboratories.
